UCP Open Day

Posted on December 2, 2020 at 12:00 PM

At our Open Day you can book 1:1 sessions with subject staff and attend talks about UCP, student finances and the UCAS application process.

Our next Open Day is on Wednesday 2nd December 2020 from 5.30pm to 8pm


At University Centre Peterborough (UCP) we pride ourselves on being able to spend time with our students and give them all the help and support they would expect when they study with us.

ABOUT OUR VIRTUAL OPEN DAY


Due to Covid-19, our campus will be closed to new visitors for this Open Day so this event will take place online, but you will still get to speak to our staff by booking 1:1 meetings at your preferred times.

1:1 SUBJECT SESSIONS


At our Virtual Open Day, you will still receive a personal experience as you will be able to speak to our course staff by booking 1:1 online sessions (lasting 15 minutes) where there will be the opportunity to ask questions.

LIVE WEBINARS


You can attend a series of live presentations as webinars which will cover Student Finances, UCAS Application Process and Student Life. These will take place every 30 minutes and last approx 20 minutes with the opportunity to ask questions.

STUDENT SUPPORT, ACCOMMODATION & EMPLOYABILITY


If you would like to speak to a member of staff about Student Support, Student Accommodation or Employability you can book 1:1 sessions for these to ensure your confidentiality. Early booking is advised to get your preferred time.

BOOKING


All 1:1 sessions must be pre-booked in advance and are subject to availability as you can book your preferred times. Last time for bookings is 5.30pm on Tuesday 1st December 2020. If all 1:1 subject sessions are fully booked, please add yourself to the waiting list and we will arrange a convenient time for you on the day or at another time.

We will send you online joining instructions by 12pm on Wednesday 2nd December 2020 . To attend online you will require access to a device with a camera (such as a smartphone, tablet, laptop or PC) so that we can talk to you and show you information on your screen.

If you have any questions about the Open Day please email radford.hudson@peterborough.ac.uk

OTHER EVENTS


If you cannot attend the Open Day on Wednesday 2nd December 2020, we also run regular Virtual 1:1 Advice Sessions. For more information about these sessions please visit www.ucp.ac.uk/events
