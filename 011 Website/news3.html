Information for students starting in September 2020

Posted on August 15, 2020 at 12:00 PM

University Centre Peterborough (UCP) are committed to providing an outstanding, supportive academic experience for all our students. The safety and wellbeing of our students and staff is at the heart of everything we do, and because of this weâ€™ll continue to follow the guidance from the OfS (Office for Students), Public Health England (PHE) and the DfE (Department of Education).

The situation we are in is unprecedented, but our primary focus is to ensure that you can progress on your course and realise your ambitions. The detail below should reassure you about the steps we are taking to ensure this.

Our commitment to teaching and learning from September 2020 will be a blended learning approach which means it will be a combination of face-to-face teaching (following social distancing measures) and online delivery.

We will be consulting with you on your preferences and responding to any queries you have in the form of a FAQ which will soon be published on our website.

Courses involving practical assessments and learning such as science-based provision may have a higher percentage of face-to-face content in semester one.

It will be our intention to return to face-to-face delivery as soon as it is safe to do so.

In the forthcoming weeks we will communicate specific course related information to applicants. This page contains the latest information which we will update with an FAQ as the situation continues to evolve.

Please stay safe and do contact us if you require further guidance. The UCP team are here to help and we are very much looking forward to you studying with us in September.
